Frequently Asked Questions

How long do your candles burn?

+

Our candles have a burn time of approximately 30-40 hours depending on the size. For best results and longest life, we recommend burning for no more than 4 hours at a time and trimming the wick to 1/4 inch before each use.

Are your candles made with natural ingredients?

+

Yes! Our candles are made with high-quality soy and coconut wax blend, lead-free cotton wicks, and phthalate-free fragrance oils. We prioritize clean burning ingredients that are better for both you and the environment.

How do I care for my gel candles?

+

Gel candles require special care: always keep them away from direct sunlight to maintain their clarity, trim the wick before each use, and never burn for more than 3 hours at a time. To clean the container after use, simply wipe with warm water.

Do you ship internationally?

+

Yes, we ship to most international destinations! International shipping rates vary by location. Please allow 10-21 business days for international orders to arrive. Any customs fees or import taxes are the responsibility of the buyer.
